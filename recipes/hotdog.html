MINT HOTDOGS

Ingredients

  • tub of mint sauce
  • mouthful of glizzies
  • crushed peas
  • toasted buns

Steps

  1. put the glizzy in the bun
  2. smash peas on the floor with your feet
  3. drop the glizzy on the floor for seasoning
  4. smother it in mint cream
  5. glizzy eating contest w/ the boys
